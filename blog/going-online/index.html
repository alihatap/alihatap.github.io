Going Online

March 13, 2018
 Tags:  digital humanities, 
Read this "Within 1 minute".
  • Do not remove this line (it will not be displayed)

After a long period of thinking about starting a research blog, I have finally madeup my mind to go online with [Victorian Naturalist] (https://www.victorian-naturalist.com). Logistics was the first question to settle, i.e. hosting. This wesite is hosted on [Github] (www.github.com). There is a learning curve to use Github pages effectively for hosting, but it shouldn’t scare the novices. Now I can have a custom domain website for as low as 10 €, which is the 2-year domain name price.

I have been very interested in digital humanities and the possible ways to integrate such a methodology in my research. So, This blog, in a way, is to overcome an aspect of digital humanities: publishing your research digitally.

The word of the day!
